based in realms of tactility, 'hands' represents objects born of explorative conglomeration (wip)

i consider myself to be a visual learner, and find that i am drawn to creation in a handsy way! i have loved (invariably so) to collect, hold, and reimagine things of many a nature, thus one might find my works to have a certain flavor, a sense of scrappiness to be observed. i appreciate mixed media for all that it is and can be, though different things strike different chords at different times.
